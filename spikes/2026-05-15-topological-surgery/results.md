## Variant A — Vertex-curvature pre-flatten guard

Diagnostic on top of v2's MST + recut output. Per Takahashi 2011: a hyperbolic vertex (corner-angle sum > 2π) needs ≥2 incident cut edges; an elliptic vertex (< 2π) needs ≥1; a parabolic vertex (= 2π) needs 0. `violations` counts vertices where v2's final cut set fails the necessary condition.
